For the first month of the server, we were focused on bug fixes and QOL. Well, you need not wait much longer, our first proper content update is right around the corner! Hack, slash and slaughter your way through four new formidable bosses. Collect the body parts of the dark lord's disciples then offer them to him at his summoning circle and you just might get his attention! Beware though, this challenging battle is only for the most well-equipped adventurers!
T80 Melee
To finish off the T80 trifecta, we'll be releasing the ranged boss not long after the melee content. The mighty Satyr, Finlas Thornbane, has reclaimed his land from the Spartans. He showed no mercy to the invaders' army -- it was a bloodbath. The cowardly general, Leonix, barely managed to escape with his life. And now, in an attempt to rally support, the disgraced general comes to you. Will you help him conquer the Guardian of the Forest? The reward might just be worth it...     Do you think you'll have the courage to risk your HCIM status vs him?
T80 Ranged
The invention expansion will introduce three new armor sets that build on Solrot. In the same way that Barrows pairs with zones 1-5 for Solrot, zones 6-8 will have their own minigame. And, of course, each of the three new zones will introduce a challenging boss fight. Work alongside your group to take down zone bosses and progress together in our all new GIM mode! This update will bring a total of ten new bosses.
Invention Expansion
You can expect us to go all out for our first raid. It will have somewhere in the ballpark of ten custom bosses, and even more custom maps. The level of detail will be insane! While the focus will be on combat mechanics, there will likely be some light puzzling/skilling elements. Oh, and grab some friends, because this will be our first true group content for all accounts!
